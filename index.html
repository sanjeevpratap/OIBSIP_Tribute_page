TimeLine

  • 1931: Born to a Tamil Muslim family in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954: Graduated in physics from Saint Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli.
  • 1955: Enrolled in Madras Institute of Technology to study aerospace engineeri
  • 1960: Joined the Aeronautical Development Establishment of DRDO as a scientist.
  • 1961: Transferred to ISRO as the project director of India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III).
  • 1980: Became the project director for India's first indigenous satellite program.
  • 2012: Launched the "What Can I Give" Movement.
  • 2015: Passed away due to a cardiac arrest in Shillong
